Privacy Policy for Notepad App

Last Updated: August 22, 2025

JoshTechApps ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how the Notepad app collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your data. By using the app, you consent to the practices described below. For questions or concerns, contact us at contactjoshtech@gmail.com.

1. Information We Collect

a. User-Generated Data

  • Notes: You can create and manage notes with the following data, stored locally in the app’s database:
    • Title and content
    • Note type (e.g., text, checklist, to-do)
    • Creation and update timestamps
    • Reminder settings (time, selected days, repeat weekly)
    • Theme preferences
    • Passwords and security questions/answers (optional, for protected notes)
    • Status flags (archived, trashed, notified, selected)
  • Calendar Interactions: The app accesses note creation and reminder dates to display notes in a calendar view.
  • User Interactions: Actions such as creating, editing, sharing, archiving, trashing, or setting reminders for notes are recorded locally to manage app functionality.

b. User Preferences

Stored locally in the app’s preferences:

  • Theme mode (light, dark, system default)
  • View type (grid or list)
  • Font size for note display
  • Sort order for notes (e.g., by modified time, created time, alphabetical)
  • Language selection (e.g., English, Arabic)
  • Auto-save toggle
  • Notification sound selection (default app sounds, custom ringtones, or none)
  • App password (optional)
  • Fingerprint authentication setting
  • Remember me setting and timestamp (for bypassing authentication for 24 hours)
  • Notification Sounds: If you select a custom ringtone, the app stores the URI of the chosen audio file.

c. Authentication Data

  • Google Sign-In: For optional backup and restore features, we collect:
    • Google account email and display name (via Firebase Authentication)
  • Passwords: App and note-specific passwords, stored locally in plain text.
  • Security Questions/Answers: For password recovery, stored locally with notes.
  • Fingerprint Authentication: Optional biometric data, managed by your device’s system.

d. Device and Permission Data

  • Permissions: The app may request:
    • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To display reminder notifications.
    • INTERNET: For Google Sign-In, Google Drive backups, and advertisements.
    • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM: For precise reminder scheduling.
    • READ_MEDIA_AUDIO or READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (pre-Android 11): To access custom notification sounds.
    • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: To reschedule reminders after device reboot.
    • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS: To ensure reliable reminders.
  • Device Data: Custom ringtone URIs and metadata (e.g., file names) when you select audio files.
  • Widgets: Interaction data from app widgets (e.g., opening notes or reminders).

e. Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to serve banner ads, which may collect:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage data for personalized ads

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Notes and Calendar: To create, edit, display, and organize notes, including in a calendar view based on creation or reminder dates.
  • Reminders: To schedule and display notifications with note title and content, using AlarmManager for one-time or recurring reminders.
  • Settings and Preferences: To personalize your experience (e.g., theme, language, font size, view type, sort order, auto-save, notification sounds).
  • Authentication: To secure app and note access via passwords, security questions, or biometric authentication.
  • Backups: To save and restore notes to/from your Google Drive account (optional, user-initiated).
  • Notifications: To show reminders, which may display note content on the lock screen, with Do Not Disturb (DND) access to ensure reliability.
  • Ads: To deliver personalized or non-personalized ads via AdMob.
  • Widgets: To provide quick access to notes, reminders, or note creation from your device’s home screen.
  • Support: To process feedback sent to contactjoshtech@gmail.com.
  • Language Support: To apply your preferred language, including right-to-left (RTL) support for languages like Arabic.

3. Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Notes, preferences, passwords, and security answers are stored locally on your device in a secure database and preferences file. Note: Passwords and security answers are stored in plain text, so please secure your device with a strong lock screen.
  • Cloud Storage: If you enable backups, notes are stored in your Google Drive appDataFolder, accessible only by your Google account and the app.
  • Retention:
    • Local data is retained until you delete it (e.g., via trash or app uninstallation).
    • Trashed notes remain in the database until permanently deleted or restored.
    • Cloud backups persist in Google Drive until you delete them.
  • Logs: Debugging logs are stored locally and not sent externally unless explicitly enabled in future updates.

4. Data Sharing

  • Google Services:
    • Firebase Authentication: Manages Google Sign-In data for backups. See Google’s Privacy Policy.
    • Google Drive API: Stores backups in your Google Drive account, encrypted during transfer.
    • Google AdMob: Serves ads and may collect data for personalization. See AdMob’s Privacy Policy.
  • User-Initiated Sharing:
    • Note Sharing: You can share note title and content via external apps (e.g., email, messaging). Be cautious when sharing sensitive information.
    • App Sharing: You can share app details via external apps or link to the Google Play Store.
    • Customer Support: Feedback sent to contactjoshtech@gmail.com may include user-provided data.
  • Widgets: Interactions with app widgets use note data locally and do not involve external sharing.
  • No Other Sharing: We do not share your data with third parties except as described above or with your explicit consent.

5. Data Security

  • Encryption: Data transferred to Google Drive is encrypted using HTTPS.
  • Local Security: Data is stored in device-protected storage, but passwords and security answers are stored in plain text, posing a risk if your device is compromised.
  • Biometric Authentication: Optional fingerprint authentication adds security on supported devices, managed by your device’s system.
  • Notifications: Reminder notifications may display note content on the lock screen, controllable via your device’s notification settings.
  • DND Access: The app requests permission to bypass Do Not Disturb mode to ensure reminder notifications are delivered reliably.

6. User Controls

  • Notes: Create, edit, lock, archive, trash, restore, or delete notes.
  • Backups: Enable or disable Google Drive backups and delete backups from your Google Drive account.
  • Settings: Toggle auto-save, select notification sounds, set or clear app passwords, enable or disable fingerprint authentication, manage remember me settings, choose themes, view types, font sizes, sort orders, and languages.
  • Ads: Opt out of personalized ads via AdMob settings or your device’s advertising preferences (e.g., reset Advertising ID).
  • Notifications: Control reminder visibility and sound via device settings.
  • Permissions: Grant or deny permissions (e.g., notifications, storage) through system prompts.
  • Data Deletion: Delete local data by moving notes to trash and permanently deleting them or uninstalling the app. Delete cloud backups via Google Drive.

7. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions to provide full functionality:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To display reminder notifications.
  • INTERNET: For Google Sign-In, Google Drive backups, and ads.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM: For precise reminder scheduling.
  • READ_MEDIA_AUDIO or READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (pre-Android 11): To access custom notification sounds.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: To reschedule reminders after device reboot.
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS: To ensure reliable reminders.

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, which may collect data as described in their privacy policies:

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages Google Sign-In for backups. See Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Google Drive API: Handles note backups and restores.
  • Google AdMob: Serves banner ads in the app. See AdMob’s Privacy Policy.

9. Your Rights

  • Access: View your notes, preferences, and backups within the app or export them via sharing or Google Drive backups.
  • Delete: Delete notes, clear preferences, or uninstall the app to remove local data. Delete backups from Google Drive to remove cloud data.
  • Opt-Out: Disable personalized ads via AdMob or device settings.
  • Privacy Requests: Contact contactjoshtech@gmail.com to exercise your rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other applicable laws (e.g., data access, deletion, or portability).

10. Children’s Privacy

If the app is intended for users under 13 (or 16 in some regions), we comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and GDPR-K by limiting data collection and requiring parental consent. Please contact us at contactjoshtech@gmail.com for details on parental controls or to provide consent.

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the app’s functionality or legal requirements. Updates will be posted in the app (via the Settings menu) and on our website (if applicable), with notifications via the Google Play Store. Your continued use of the app after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact Us

For questions, concerns, or to exercise your privacy rights, email us at contactjoshtech@gmail.com.
