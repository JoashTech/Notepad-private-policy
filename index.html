Privacy Policy for Notepad App

Last Updated: 04 November, 2025

JoshTechApps ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how the Notepad app handles and processes your information. By using the app, you consent to these practices. For inquiries, contact us at contactjoshtech@gmail.com.

1. Information We Process

a. User Content and App Data

  • Notes Content: Text, checklists, and to-do items you create
  • App Preferences: Theme selections, language settings, font sizes, and display preferences
  • Reminder Settings: Scheduled notifications and recurrence patterns
  • Security Settings: App access passwords and biometric authentication preferences

b. Technical and Analytics Information

To maintain and improve app performance, we utilize the following services:

  • Firebase Analytics: Processes aggregated usage data to help us improve app functionality
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash reports and diagnostic information to maintain app stability

c. Advertising Information

  • Google AdMob: May process device identifiers for advertisement delivery
  • Vungle & Unity Ads: Additional advertising services that may process technical information

d. Backup and Sync Services

  • Google Drive Integration: Optional cloud backup functionality stores your notes in your personal Google Drive account
  • Authentication Data: Google account email for secure backup access

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Core Functionality: Create, edit, display, and organize notes with reminder capabilities
  • Personalization: Apply theme, language, font, and display preferences
  • Security: Secure app access with passwords or biometric authentication
  • Backup Services: Enable optional cloud backups to Google Drive
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Advertising: Support app development through advertisement display

3. Data Storage and Retention

Local Storage

  • All note content and app preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Trashed notes are retained locally until permanently deleted

Cloud Storage

  • Backup data in Google Drive persists until you delete it through the app or directly in Google Drive
  • Analytics and crash data are retained according to Google's data retention policies
Backup Management: You can delete cloud backups at any time using the "Delete Backup" option in the app's backup settings section.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Services:
    • Firebase Authentication for secure sign-in
    • Google Drive API for cloud backups
    • Firebase Analytics for usage insights
    • Firebase Crashlytics for stability monitoring
    • Google AdMob for advertisement delivery
  • Advertising Partners:
    • Vungle (Liftoff) for additional advertising services
    • Unity Ads for advertisement delivery

5. User Controls and Choices

  • Local Data Management: Delete individual notes or clear all data by uninstalling the app
  • Cloud Backup Control: Enable/disable backups or delete them using the "Delete Backup" feature
  • Analytics Opt-Out: Control through device settings or by uninstalling the app
  • Advertising Preferences: Manage personalized advertising through device settings
  • Permissions Control: Grant/deny app permissions through system prompts

6. Data Security

  • Local Protection: Data stored on your device utilizes built-in security features
  • Cloud Security: Google Drive backups use industry-standard encryption
  • Biometric Safety: Fingerprint authentication handled by your device's secure hardware
  • Network Security: All cloud transfers use HTTPS encryption

7. Data Deletion Requests

You can request deletion of your data through the following methods:

  • Local Data: Uninstall the app or use the trash feature to permanently delete notes
  • Cloud Backups: Use the "Delete Backup" option in the app or manually delete through Google Drive
  • Account Data: Email contactjoshtech@gmail.com for complete account deletion requests

8. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions, data deletion requests, or additional information, contact us at: contactjoshtech@gmail.com
